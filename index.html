Mission Statement

OurScene, INC. is dedicated to revolutionizing the live music industry by creating a dynamic platform that connects local bands with promoters, venues, and enthusiastic fans. Our mission is to empower emerging artists, boost the local music scene, and enrich the lives of music enthusiasts.

Company Overview

OurScene, LLC. was founded by Bryan Cox and Nakoa Arnold, passionate music enthusiasts and longtime musicians, with extensive experience in event management and technology development. Headquartered in Arlinton, Texas, OurScene aims to bridge the gap between talented local bands and the vibrant music ecosystem in the region.

The Real Revolution


OurScene is a digital revolution in the local music scene, fundamentally transforming how artists, venues, promoters, and fans connect. By addressing the most pressing challenges in the industry, OurScene is not just a platform but a movement, rekindling the excitement and accessibility of local shows. Its intuitive design and innovative features bridge the gap between emerging artists and music enthusiasts, simplifying the process of discovering, booking, and attending live events. In a world where the local music scene often struggles for visibility, OurScene stands as a beacon of innovation, making it not just easy but delightful for fans to immerse themselves in the vibrant tapestry of local talent. This is more than a service; it's a revitalization of community spirit, where every chord struck and note played resonates with the collective heartbeat of local music fans and artists.
